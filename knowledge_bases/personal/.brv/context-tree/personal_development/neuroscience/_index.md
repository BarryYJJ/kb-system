# Personal Development: Neuroscience

This domain focuses on the biological foundations of executive function and cognitive optimization, specifically targeting the enhancement of the prefrontal cortex (PFC).

### Prefrontal Cortex (PFC) Optimization
The core focus is on strengthening the PFC to improve flexible planning and emotional management. Strategies are categorized into internal biological maintenance and external cognitive support systems.

*   **Executive Function Strategies**:
    *   **Goal Alignment**: Defining clear long-term goals is the primary driver for PFC activation.
    *   **Signal Filtering**: A critical operational rule (Rule 1) involves deliberately ignoring low-level biological signals—such as hunger or laziness—once basic health requirements are met to overcome cognitive inertia.
    *   **External Augmentation**: Utilization of "external memory" tools (e.g., writing/documentation) to offload cognitive load and resist PFC fatigue.

*   **Architectural Prerequisites**:
    *   **Energy Foundation**: Physical health, sustained through consistent sleep and exercise, is documented as the essential energy provider for PFC functionality.
