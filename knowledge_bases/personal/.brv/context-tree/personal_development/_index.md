# Neuroscience: Cognitive Optimization

Focuses on biological foundations of executive function, specifically **Prefrontal Cortex (PFC)** enhancement for flexible planning and emotional regulation.

### PFC Optimization & Executive Function
*   **Goal Alignment**: Primary driver for PFC activation via clear long-term objectives.
*   **Signal Filtering (Rule 1)**: Deliberate suppression of low-level biological signals (hunger, laziness) once health basics are met to bypass cognitive inertia.
*   **Cognitive Offloading**: Use of "external memory" (documentation/writing) to mitigate PFC fatigue and reduce cognitive load.

### Structural Prerequisites
*   **Energy Foundation**: Physical health, prioritized through sleep and exercise, is the mandatory architecture for PFC functionality.
