## Narrative
### Structure
Core concept: Bet on extremes. Implementation: 90% conservative (bonds, cash), 10% aggressive (startups, derivatives). AI Application: AI vs physical assets.

### Highlights
1. Limited downside, unlimited upside. 2. Avoids medium risk. 3. Promotes antifragility.

### Rules
Rule 1: Do not bet in the middle.
Rule 2: 90% must be extremely conservative.
Rule 3: 10% must be extremely aggressive.

### Examples
AI Era: One end is AI (LLMs), the other end is physical assets (energy, space, biology). Avoid the "AI + traditional industry" middle ground.
