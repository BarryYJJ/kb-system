## Narrative
### Structure
Summary of 10 key takeaways from a 2-day investor conference at Data Center World London.

### Dependencies
Growth constrained by power availability, grid access, fiber supply, and product lead times.

### Highlights
Direct-to-chip liquid cooling dominant long-term; Fiber supply-demand imbalance to last 2-3 years; Modular solutions and end-to-end providers (Eaton, Schneider) favored for speed to market.

### Rules
Rule 1: Specification and delivery capability are more important than price in the current market.
Rule 2: 800V DC architecture is economically viable only for ultra-large campuses (100-200MW+) and extreme rack densities.

### Examples
Companies covered: ABB, Carrier, Eaton, Fujikura, Schneider Electric, Siemens Energy, etc. Fiber price spike in China: $2/km to $9/km.
