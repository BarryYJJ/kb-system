## Narrative
### Structure
The analysis categorizes the market into Internet Giants (Byte, Alibaba, Baidu, Tencent) and Emerging AI Labs (MiniMax, Zhipu, Moonshot, DeepSeek, StepFun).

### Dependencies
Internet giants rely on existing ecosystems (social/e-commerce) for data and distribution; AI labs rely on technical innovation (AGI) and niche enterprise services.

### Highlights
Key highlights include the strong multimodal capabilities of ByteDance, the vertical industry focus of Baidu, and the pivot of Moonshot Kimi towards enterprise productivity. Video generation is noted as a high-potential area for short dramas and e-commerce.
