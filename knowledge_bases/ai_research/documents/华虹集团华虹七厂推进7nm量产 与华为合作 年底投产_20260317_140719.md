Hua Hong Group, China's No. 2 chipmaker, is preparing 7nm production through its Huali unit, a major milestone for Beijing's tech self-sufficiency. Collaborating with Huawei, Huali aims for initial production by year-end. GPU designer Biren is already testing prototypes on the line after losing TSMC access. Currently producing 22/28nm chips at Fab 6, Huali follows SMIC as the second domestic firm capable of 7nm, a technology critical for advanced artificial intelligence chips.
