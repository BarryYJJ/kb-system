# Domain: ai_china

The Chinese AI landscape in early 2026 is defined by a strategic divergence between established internet giants and emerging AI labs, alongside a massive expansion into the global LLM token export market driven by aggressive pricing and domestic infrastructure.

### Strategic Market Segmentation
The market is categorized into two primary groups with distinct operational focuses:
*   **Internet Giants (ByteDance, Alibaba, Baidu, Tencent):** Focus on domestic consumer "AI Super-apps" to capture new traffic. Key players include **ByteDance (Doubao)** leveraging TikTok/social data, **Alibaba** integrating Qwen with DingTalk, and **Baidu (Ernie)** focusing on vertical industry efficiency (Finance, Healthcare).
*   **Emerging AI Labs (MiniMax, Zhipu, Moonshot, DeepSeek, StepFun):** Prioritize enterprise services, coding, and autonomous agent capabilities. Notable shifts include **Moonshot Kimi** pivoting toward enterprise productivity (Docs, PPT) and **Zhipu AI** scaling via 2B/2G local deployments.

### LLM Token Export Strategy
China has rapidly increased its global inference market share, notably rising from 2% to 39% on platforms like OpenRouter within 15 months.
*   **Infrastructure & Cost:** Strategy relies on domestic inference clusters (utilizing **RTX 5090** GPUs and low-cost electricity) coupled with distilled data. This allows for API pricing approximately **1/6th** of the global average.
*   **Operational Rules:** Marketing and front-end infrastructure remain overseas, while compute remains domestic. Exported data is primarily coding-related to mitigate sensitivity risks.
*   **Key Dependencies:** Heavy reliance on domestic cloud providers (**Volcengine**, **Alibaba Cloud**) and anticipated Southeast Asian subsea cable expansions for bandwidth.

### Key Technological Pillars
*   **Multimodal & Video:** Strong focus on video generation efficiency for e-commerce and short dramas (notably by ByteDance).
*   **Agentic Capabilities:** Increasing emphasis on AI agents and high-context productivity tools.
*   **Inference Efficiency:** A shift toward domestic infrastructure for cost optimization despite a typical inference latency of ~100 seconds for exported tokens.
