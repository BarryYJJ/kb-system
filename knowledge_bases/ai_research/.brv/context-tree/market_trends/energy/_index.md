## Key Knowledge Entries

### [Oil Price Impact on US-Iran Conflict](oil_price_impact_on_us_iran_conflict.md)
Analysis of oil as a bargaining chip within the US-Iran geopolitical framework.
- **Workflow:** Conflict $\rightarrow$ Supply Threat $\rightarrow$ Price Surge $\rightarrow$ US Inflation $\rightarrow$ Political Pressure $\rightarrow$ Policy Shift.
- **Strategic Geography:** The **Strait of Hormuz** is identified as a primary leverage point, accounting for **20% of global oil supply**.
- **Economic Thresholds:** 
    - **Price Red Line:** US political/economic tolerance erodes at **$90–$100/barrel**.
    - **Inflation Impact:** Every $10 increase in oil price correlates to a **~0.2% rise in US CPI**.
    - **Policy Rule:** If prices hit the $90–$100 range (pushing inflation above 3%), strategic gains are outweighed by domestic costs, forcing US intervention or resolution.
- **Dependencies:** US conflict tolerance is strictly tied to domestic inflation and election cycles.
